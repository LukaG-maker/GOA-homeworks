All Eyez on Me | About

All Eyez on Me is the fourth studio album by American rapper 2Pac and the final to be released during his lifetime. Artist: Tupac Shakur Genres: Hip-hop, Gangsta rap, West Coast hip-hop, G-funk, Rap music, Hardcore hip hop Release date: February 13, 1996 Label: Interscope Records Length: 132:20 Studio: Can-Am Studios (Tarzana, Los Angeles)
